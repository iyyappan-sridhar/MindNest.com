How Therapy Works

  1. Initial assessment: We listen — goals, history, and current concerns.
  2. Personalized plan: Therapist builds simple, practical steps for progress.
  3. Weekly sessions: Short exercises, reflection and skill-building each week.
  4. Review & support: Regular check-ins to keep momentum and adjust plan.

Who benefits?

Anyone dealing with stress, low mood, sleep trouble, concentration issues, or relationship strain. Therapy is for support, not just crisis.
