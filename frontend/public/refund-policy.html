Refund Policy

At MindNest Counselling, we strive to offer transparent, fair, and client-friendly policies. Since our services involve time-based professional counselling, certain refund rules apply.

1. Session Booking & Payments

All payments made for counselling sessions (via Razorpay or other modes) are considered confirmed bookings. Once the booking is successful, the therapist reserves the session time exclusively for you.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are provided only under the following conditions:

  • ❗ **Payment was deducted but booking did not get confirmed** due to a technical error.
  • ❗ **Therapist cancels the session** and no alternative time is available.
  • ❗ **Duplicate payment** made by the user for the same session.

No refunds will be issued in these cases:

  • ⚠ User misses the session or joins late.
  • ⚠ User is unable to attend without prior notice.
  • ⚠ Session is already completed.

3. Rescheduling Policy

If you cannot attend the booked session, you may reschedule **at least 4 hours before** the session time. Rescheduling is free and allowed only once per booking.
